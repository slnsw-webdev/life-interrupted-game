Games
Rules

1. Each game consists of 12 Questions and Answers - The questions appear at the top of the page, and the answers appear at the bottom.

2. Read the questions at the top and choose the answer which you think is correct. Drag and drop your answer over the questions.

3. If the answer is correct, the card will turn green - if it is incorrect, you will have to try again.

4. Have fun! see how quickly you can complete it for an extra challenge!
